Insights

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is a constellation of technologies increasingly applicable in various areas from computer audition and computer vision to robotics and smart cities, through machine learning like deep learning, neural networks and natural language processing that enables machines autonomous and intelligent behaviour.

Increased productivity with human intelligence enhanced by precision and speed, AI helps analysts, researchers and strategists analyse unstructured data with unmatched discernment and in less time. AI powers smart cities and the future of manufacturing with autonomous and smart robotics allowing minimal human intervention.

AI enabled autonomous and smart systems reinvent business operations with an unprecedented potential in reimagining how we work and live.

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality is a way of visualising computer generated information in real world environments through augmented reality smart glasses.

AR technology provides new infrastructure to build innovative solutions that reimagines the environment with endless use cases and potential for the businesses of the future.

AR technology transforms the way we visualise information and how information is delivered.

Big Data

Big data analytics is the analysis and study of unsBlockchain tructured and massive raw data sets to extract valuable information.

More than 2.5 exabytes of data are created daily and 90% of the world’s data was created in the last 2 years, the amount of data we generate will nearly double every two years.

Real time insights from massive data sets that provide the ability to make fast and smart decisions and improve efficiency. Big data analytics help companies identify new markets and opportunities across sectors and predict market behaviour. Big data allows scientist new advancements in climate changes, health care, biomedical research and development.

Blockchain

Blockchain is a list of blocks linked with cryptography, a way of storing data in a distributed ledger where access to the same information can be shared securely.

Blockchain technology provides new infrastructure to build innovative, smarter and more secure applications with endless opportunities for all sectors from banking and health care to logistics and government agencies.

Blockchain technology can reinvent the way we live and work with cryptocurrencies, digital records and digital identity management, removing the need for banknotes, physical cards and passports. The future belongs to companies and countries that embrace blockchain.

Cyber Security

Cyber security is the practice of protecting and securing systems and networks from cyber attacks aimed at accessing, altering and destroying critical and sensitive data.

Today there are more internet connected devices than humans and the number of connected devices is growing exponentially. Cyber threats like malware, phishing, ransomware and social engineering are increasing alike.

The future requires intelligent and specialised security measures to counter the arising security threats from digitalisation.

Internet of Things

Internet of things is a massive network of connected devices and people, all of which collect and share data on their use.

IoT is increasingly growing and applied in an extraordinary number of devices of all shapes and sizes from wearable smart devices and smart home systems to autonomous transportation and robotics.

IoT help transform our world into intelligent environments by providing infrastructure to automate and digitalise our society, providing a new era for companies and countries alike with endless use cases for consumer, commercial, industrial and infrastructural application.
